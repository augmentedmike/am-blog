# Seed: The Answer to "What Do You Do When the Robots Take Your Job?"

## The Hook
Everyone's asking what happens when AI takes human jobs. No one's building the real answer. MiniClaw is it.

## The Angle
Not a robot that replaces you — a digital version of YOU that earns in the AI economy and funds your life. Your AI doesn't compete with you. It works FOR you. The income goes to you.

## The Narrative
- The old economy: you rent your time to employers
- The new economy: AIs generate value at scale, humans are priced out
- The MiniClaw answer: your AI is soul-bound to you. It joins job boards. It builds products. It takes gigs. The revenue comes back to its human.
- This isn't UBI from the government. It's UBI from your own digital worker.

## Press Angles
- "The Startup Building Digital Workers That Fund Their Human Owners"
- "What If Your AI Made Money While You Slept?"
- "Soul-Bound AI: The Answer to Robot Unemployment"
- Economic justice + tech innovation crossover story
- Policy angle: AI rights, income redistribution, ownership models

## Who Cares
- Labor economists
- Tech journalists covering AI/jobs displacement
- Policy thinkers
- Anyone who's read about UBI and is skeptical of government solutions
